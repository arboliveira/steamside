Some cool things you'll be able to do...

  Get a start screen of your own with customizable Continue, Favorites and Search

  Organize your games in multiple collections like those in Greenlight and the Steam Workshop

  Select alternate display pictures and command aliases for your games

  Limit the games each of your kids can see and play

  Access detailed play history and statistics, and share them online whenever you want

  Invite your friends with a link to auto-install Steam and play their very first game gifted from you

  Do quests and earn achievements: play Steam itself as a game!
